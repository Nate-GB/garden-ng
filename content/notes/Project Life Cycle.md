Project management is performed in a series of phases that are executed to apply knowledge, skills, tools, and techniques to the project activities to meet the project requirements. Per CompTIA, there are 5 phases of project management:

1. [Discovery and Concept](notes/Discovery%20and%20Concept)
2. [Initiating](notes/Initiating)
3. [Planning](notes/Planning)
4. [Executing](notes/Executing)
5. [Closing](notes/Closing)
